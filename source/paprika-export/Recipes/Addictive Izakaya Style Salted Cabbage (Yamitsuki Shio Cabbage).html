Addictive Izakaya Style Salted Cabbage (Yamitsuki Shio Cabbage)

Asian, Full Veggies

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 5 mins Source: sudachirecipes.com

Ingredients

1 ½ tbsp toasted sesame oil

1 tbsp sesame seeds

½ tsp Chinese-style chicken bouillon powder

¼ tsp salt

¼ tsp garlic paste or grated garlic clove

¼ tsp ground black pepper

100 g green cabbage spring cabbage, sweetheart cabbage or similar

chili threads optional, to garnish

Directions

Take a large mixing bowl and add 1 ½ tbsp toasted sesame oil, 1 tbsp sesame seeds, ½ tsp Chinese-style chicken bouillon powder, ¼ tsp salt, ¼ tsp garlic paste and ¼ tsp ground black pepper. Mix thoroughly.

Wash 100 g green cabbage and cut it into rough pieces.

Add the cabbage to the bowl and rub the seasoning over the leaves, making sure it's evenly distributed. (Wear gloves if you have sensitive skin or any cuts on your hands.)

Transfer to a serving dish and top with chili threads (optional).

Enjoy as a side or snack!
