Portuguese Favas

★★★★★

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 30 mins Servings: 8 Source: allrecipes.com

Ingredients

5 tablespoons olive oil

3 large onions, chopped (2 mega large)

3 portuguese sausages / louisiana hot sausage

2 cloves garlic, minced

2 teaspon red pepper flakes

salt to taste

½ teaspoon black pepper

2 tablespoon paprika

¼ cup tomato sauce

2 cups hot water (broth ok)

3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

bowl of fresh fava beans peeled (one grand saladier of unpeeled fava beans see pictures)

Description

MODIFIED BY TOM

Directions

Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Sautee slced sausages. Saute onion and garlic until golden brown. Stir in red pepper flakes, tomato sauce, hot water, parsley, salt, pepper and paprika. Bring to a boil, add faba beans, reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes. Can mijote longer.

Remove from heat and let stand for several minutes to allow flavors to meld.
