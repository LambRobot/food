Steph’s Butternut Squash Thai Curry

★★★★★

Asian

Ingredients

1 yellow onion minced

3-6 garlic cloves crushed

1-2 inches ginger grated

1 butternut squash cubed

16 oz mushrooms sliced

3 tbsp curry paste

2 tsp fish sauce

2 cans coconut milk

1 pack chicken thighs cut in pieces

one bunch kale

1/2 tsp rice vinegar (/to taste)

Directions

Soften minced onion in veggie oil or coconut oil or butter
Add garlic and ginger, until fragrant
Add curry paste, until fragrant
Add mushrooms
Start cooking chicken on the side (might be ok to add with butternut squash instead - no need for browning it will just dry it out)
Once mushrooms start looking through, add coconut milk
Add butternut
Once butternut is almost cooked, add fish sauce and chicken
Once everything is cooked, add kale
Add rice vinegar
