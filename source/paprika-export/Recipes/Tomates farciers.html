Tomates farciers

Prep Time: 15min Cook Time: 45min Servings: 4 Source: belgian recipy

Ingredients

For the farce:

- 1.5 lb of ground meat (porc/beef mix)

- 1 scallot minced

- 1 tbsp mustard

- 1 egg (separate white and yolk)

- 1 slice of break dipped in milk

- garlic powder

- parsley

- 6 tomatoes (to confirm)


For puree:

- Potatoes

- Milk

- Nutmeg

Directions

To prepare the meat:
- Mix all ingredients together with the egg white beaten stiff (add salt to stiffen)
- Pre-heat oven at 390F
- Empty the tomatoes, fill them with the farce, add butter on top.
- Cook for 3/4h
