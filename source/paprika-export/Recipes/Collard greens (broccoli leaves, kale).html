Collard greens (broccoli leaves, kale)

★★★★★

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 35 mins Difficulty: Easy Servings: 3 Source: Youtube + my twist

Ingredients

one big collander full of brocolli leaves, broccoli head or flowers, kale leaves

1 onion

2 garlic cloves

2 laddle of broth

Directions

De-stems the leaves (hold the end of the stem from one hand and with the other hand press betweem thumb and indexalong the stem to rip the leaves out).

Dice the onions. Rince the leaves and shake them a bit to get tid of exvess water. On a cutting noard, roll the leaves together like a burrito, then cut the bunch in 2“ rolls.

Heat olive oil and butter in a sautee pan, put in the onion and press the garlic in. Stir for a moment then add the chopped greens. Sautee without a lid at medium height for 5-7 min. Add salt and pepper.

Add two ladles of broth to the pan, cover and let simmer at low heat for 15 min. Taste yo make sure the texture is correct amd serve!
