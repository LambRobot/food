Lambot awesome risotto mushroom and asparagus

★★★★★

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 40 mins Servings: 6 Source: Self

Ingredients

2 cups arborio rice

1 big onion chopped

2 garlic clove

7 cups broth (use home made lamb broth!)

1 box mushroom

1 pack asparagus chopped in ¼ inch bits

1/2 tablespoon herbes de provence

1 couple puff of cumin

½ tablespoom thyme

3 fingers of white wine

1 big handfull of grated parmesan (use old stinky parmesan)

olive oil

butter


Directions

In a side pot, heat the broth until almost boiling, then cut the heat and keep it aside warm.

In a big pot (celle pour la sauce bolo) Heat olive oil, throw in the chopped onions and the 2 garlic cloves crushed ar medium-high heat. Stir until the onions are whitened.

Throw in the chopped mushrooms, add a big chunk of butter and put thyme in. Salt and pepper quite a good amount. Keep the lid off (never use the lid in this recipy) wtir the mushroom and and onions and butter ti coat eveything wel. Get it going at high heat and evaportate a good amount of the water (spend 2-3 min just reducing the juixe ar high heat while stiring). Empty the contents in a large bowl and set aside.

Using the same pot, add big chunk of butter at high heat. Once melted throw in the rice, add olive oil to make sure eveything is coated. Stir for 2min to lightly toast the whole thing.

Put 3 fingers worth of white wine in the pot (still at high heat). If cocky, flambee the stuff. Add the herbes de provence. Add salt.

Once wine is reduced, add the broth one ladle at a time and steer. This will take ~15min, one ladle at a time. Stir so it doesnt attach at the bottom. Avoid putting too mich liquid.

When you are about 3 ladle out of emptying the broth, add back the mushrooms and onions.

When you are about 2 ladles out of emptying the broth, add the asparagus. Stir well. Coat them fucker good. Taste and adjust salt / pepper.

When about 1 ladle out of emptying the broth, add some of the parmesan, the stinky bits, to get that taste going. Add aome cumin puffs, adjust salt / pepper. Keep cooking for a few minutea until ita super creamy.

Serve with additional parmesan.
