Zucchini & butternut squash sautee

★★★★★

Prep Time: 10 mins Servings: 5

Ingredients

1 butternut squash

2-3 zucchinis

1 onion

2 garlic clove

oil

butter

1 dash rosemary

3 dash epices emilie

1/2 tsp thyme

1/2 tsp oregano

salt

pepper

Directions

Cut the zucchinis and butternut squash in small dices. Chop the inion roughly.

Sautee the smashed garlic in olive oil (use the demeyere a grand fond). When the onions are whitened, add the other veggies with a big chunk of butter. Add the spices, put at medium heat, stir well then cover. Keep xovered for 5 min.

Remove lid, turn at high heat, keep on stirring to reduce some of the liquid. The zicchini should be crispy and butternut to start to break down. Can cut the heat and keep covered for 10 min to increase creaminess
