Beer Chuck roast and lentils slow cook

★★★★★

Slow cooking

Prep Time: 25 mins Cook Time: 5 hrs Difficulty: Easy Servings: 6 Source: Lambot

Ingredients

1 chuck roast (costco makes pack with 2 large, take one of the piece)

1 bottle of brown beer (chouffe or equivalent)

1 bunch of carrots (when chopped, aame volume as meat)

1 cup of lentils (+-1/2)

1 diced tomato can

8 stick of celery

2 large onions

5 garlic cloves

1 box of beef broth

1 tablespoon dry parsley

1 tablespoon thyme

1/2 tablespoon rosemary

1/2 tablespoon white pepper

1 tablespoon red pepper flake or 1/2 tablespoon melange epice pimente de emilie

1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce

1 dash apple cider vinegar


sea salt

fresh pepper

Directions

Cut the chuck roast in dice of up to 1” side, keep some of the fat with it. Season straight away with frah pepper and sea salt.

Chop coarsly the onions. Peel ans chop the carrots. Cut the celery sticks.

Put a layer of olive oil at bottom of slow cooker, add all the ingredients (beef, carrot, celery, tomato, lentils, onion, garlic). Add all the seasonning, broth, beer. Add a bit more salt.

Put the slow cooker on release pressure, and set the slow cook on 5h. Try every 3 hours to adjust sewsonning, dont be too aggressive on it, flavors develop as you cook. Allow it to rest 20 min before serving. The beef should taste like beer.
