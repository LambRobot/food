Poisson Parmentier

Prep Time: 15 Cook Time: 40 Servings: 4 Source: Belgian recipy

Ingredients

0.75 lb of cod or haddock

0.45 lb of mushroom

7 oz of peeled tomato

4.5 oz of shrimps

1 onion diced

2 oz butter

1.5 oz of flour

1/2 cup of milk

7 fl oz of white wine

2.5 oz of mousseline

Directions

-Cut the fish in big pieces an put them in a oven-safe dish. Cover with mushrooms, tomato and a bit of tomato juice. Sprinkle with shrimps.
- In a pan, whiten the onion with the butter. Add the flour and without stopping to mix, add the milk slowly. Let cook at low heat for 5 min while mixing.
- Prepare the mousseline according to the recipy. Salt and pepper. Add mousseline on top of the fish. Add pieces of butter on the top.
- Bake for 30 min at 355F. The top must be golden.
