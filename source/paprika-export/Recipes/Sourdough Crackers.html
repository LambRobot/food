Sourdough Crackers

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes Servings: Yield: about 60 crackers Source: baked-theblog.com

Ingredients

½ cup (75g) all-purpose flour

½ cup (70g) whole wheat flour

¾ tsp (3g) sea salt

1 cup (200g) unfed sourdough starter

¼ cup (53g) olive oil, plus more for brushing

Flaky salt and za'atar for topping

Directions

Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).

In a large bowl, mix together the all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, salt, sourdough starter, and olive oil to make a smooth dough.

Knead for about 30 seconds, or until the dough looks consistent and feels pliable.

Divide the dough in half. Place each half onto a piece of parchment paper (or reusable baking liner), and use your hands to flatten then into small rectangles.

Using a rolling pin, roll each piece of dough to about 2mm (1/16″) thick. The dough will have misshapen edges, that’s normal.

Brush the rolled out dough with olive oil and use a pizza cutter or other knife to cut the crackers into 4cm (1.5″) squares. Top with sea salt and za’atar or other toppings.

Transfer the crackers with the parchment paper to two large baking sheets (or bake one batch at a time).

Bake for 20-25 minutes, rotating the pans halfway through, or until the squares are starting to brown around the edges.

Cool fully on the baking sheets. The crackers can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for a week.
