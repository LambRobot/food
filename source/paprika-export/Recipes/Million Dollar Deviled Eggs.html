Million Dollar Deviled Eggs

★★★★★

Servings: Serving: 1 Source: sugarspunrun.com

Ingredients

12 large eggs

¼ cup mayo (55g)

1 Tablespoon butter softened to room temperature

2 teaspoons yellow mustard

2 teaspoons dijon mustard

2 teaspoons sweet gherkin pickle juice (i took my pickle juice drom the big pickles)

1 teaspoon sugar

⅛ teaspoon salt (need more)

⅛ teaspoon pepper

dash Tabasco sauce optional (+dash hot sauce)

paprika for sprinkling

bacon pieces optional

Directions

How To Hard Boil Eggs

Place eggs in a large saucepan and cover with water.

Transfer to stovetop over high heat until water begins to boil.

IF STEAM THE EGGS, STEAM FOR 13MIN+

Boil for one minute, cover with lid, and remove from heat. Allow to sit for 17 minutes and then drain and transfer to an ice bath.

Peel eggs and set aside.

How To Make Deviled Eggs

Slice eggs in half lengthwise.

Remove yolks and transfer to a medium-sized bowl.

Add mayo, butter, mustards, pickle juice, sugar, salt, pepper, and Tabasco sauce. Use a fork to mash well. PUT IN THE SMALL HAND BLENDER breville THINGY, it fits all eggs and ingredients.

Spoon filling into each egg white. Sprinkle with paprika and bacon (if using) and serve.

If desired, top with a small slice of sweet gherkin pickle, as seen in pictures.
