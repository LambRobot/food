Bacon & Boursin Madeleines

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Servings: 18 madeleines Source: bakingwithnessa.com

Ingredients

1/4 cup (50 g) butter (melted)

2 eggs

1 tbsp warm water (boiled)

2 tbsp flour (sifted)

1/2 cup (50 g) almond flour

1 tsp (5 g) baking powder

3.5 oz (100 g) bacon (crumbled)

5.2 oz (150 g) Boursin cheese (whipped)

Directions

Preheat oven at 350F/180C.

Place butter in a small sauce pan and heat on low until melted. Set aside.

Separate egg yolks from egg whites. Gently whisk egg whites with a fork.

In the mixing bowl, whisk egg yolks with one tbsp of warm water, until fluffy. Add flour, almond flour, baking powder, melted butter and egg whites.

With a spatula, combine crumbled bacon and Boursin cheese.

Pour your batter into the flexipan® by spreading gently until smooth.

Bake for 15 minutes at 350F/180C. (Every oven heats differently, monitor your madeleines from 10min)

Once your madeleines are out of the oven, let them cool down for about 5min. Then, Unmold, Serve and Enjoy!
