Spicy Fava Beans Sausage Stewy Appetizer / Dish

★★★★★

Ingredients

~30 fava beans blanched for 1 min and peeled

1 knakwurst or spicy portuguese sausage

½ onion

1 large garlic

½ cup porto

1 cup broth

1 tsp smoked paprika

1/2 tsp ground pepper

1/4 tsp or more of salt

1 tsp spicy oil (skip if using spicy sausages)

1/4 tsp apple cider vinegar

MAYBE TRY SOME TOMATOES WITH IT?

Description

Recipe i invented, supposed to have portuguese influence. Yield for 2 small bowl for a good appetizer size. Its filling !! Careful some people camnot eat fava beans.

Directions

Slice the sausage and sautee them on medium heat in spicy oil (or regular oil). Add onions and garlic and sauté for a few minutes until weeping. Deglaze in porto, add spices. Once reduced add fava beans, broth, vinegar and cook with lid for 10 minutes until liquid reduced and creamy. Adjust seasonning. I had some garlic powder added last time.
