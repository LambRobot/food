Smoked Salmon Mini Muffins

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Servings: 18 mini bites Source: bakingwithnessa.com

Ingredients

4 eggs

3 1/2 oz (100 g) butter (softened)

2 oz (50 g) mustard

Salt/Pepper

2 tbsp fines herbs

4 1/4 oz (120 g) flour (sifted)

1 1/4 tsp (6 g) baking powder

4 slices smoked salmon (chopped)

3 1/2 oz (100 g) grated cheese

Directions

Preheat oven to 350F/180C.

In the mixing bowl, whisk together eggs, butter, mustard, salt/pepper and herbs.

Using a spatula, add flour, baking powder, smoked salmon and grated cheese.

Pour your batter into the flexipan® with the mini ladle.

Bake for 20 minutes at 350F/180C. (Every oven heats differently, monitor your mini bites from 15min)

Once your mini bites are out of the oven, let them cool down for about 10min. Then, Unmold, Serve and Enjoy!
