Prosciutto and arugula pizza

Prep Time: 4 Cook Time: 10 Difficulty: Medium Servings: 10

Ingredients

4 pizza dough

2 garlic cloves sliced in two

9 oz mozzarella minced

8 cherry tomatoes sliced in four

5 oz prosciutto

2.5 oz arugula

4tsp balasmic vinegar

salt and pepper

Directions

Rub the pizza dough with garlic.

Put the pizza dough on oven paper. Dispose mozzarella, tomatoes and bake for 10 min in a oven pre-heated at 400F until the the dough is golden.

Dispose the prosciutto and arugula on the pizzas. Add some balsamic vinegar to taste. Serve.
