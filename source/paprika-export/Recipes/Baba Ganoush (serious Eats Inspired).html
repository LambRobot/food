Baba Ganoush (serious Eats Inspired)

★★★★★

Prep Time: 25 mins Cook Time: 30 mins Servings: 4 to 6 servings Source: seriouseats.com

Ingredients

3 medium Italian eggplants (about 2 pounds/900g total), / 5 homegrown heirloom eggplant

2 tablespoons (30 ml) lemon juice from 1 lemon, plus more as desired

2 medium cloves garlic, pressed

3 tablespoons (45 ml) tahini

1/3 cup (80 ml) extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for serving

1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley leaves optional

Kosher salt

Directions

SMOKER: traeger at 250F, smoke the eggplants whole for 90 min (i flipped them halfway through).

Let rest 20min (or until not too hot). Slice eggplant open and empty content into a food processor bowl (the small one that goes on top). Discard skins and burned bits.

Add lemon juice, tahini, pressed garlic, olive oil, 1/2 tsp salt. Pulse in fold processor. Texture should be pale and creamy.

Taste and adjust salt / olive oil / lemon juice.

Eat with pita, or pita chips :-)
