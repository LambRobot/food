Couscous!

★★★★★

Prep Time: 35 mins Cook Time: 2 hrs Difficulty: Easy Servings: 6-835

Ingredients

- 2 cup chicken broth

- 1 large onion

- 3 cloves garlic

- 1 turnip

- 3 medium zucchini

- 6 large carrots

- 1 red bell pepper

- 1 can chick peas

- 2 cans crushed tomatoes

- 1½-2 tbsp raz el anouth


for the chicken

- one pack chicken tighs

- 1 cup all purpose flour

- 0.5 tbsp paprika

- 0.5 tbsp cumin

- salt & pepper

Directions

Prep the veggies: Dice the onion, Cut the turnip in square and the rest in slices.

Mix the all purpose flour, paprika, cumin, salt and pepper in a large dish. Roll the chicken in the mexture to coat them on all sides.

In a large pot (dutch oven) heat up on high a few tbsp of vegetable oil. Brown the chicken on each side a few at a time to not overcrowd the pan. Put them aside.

Pre heat the oven at 375F.

In the pan, still on high, heat all the veggies in the leftovet oil (could try also just garlic and onion them the rest), ass the ras el anouth. After 5-8 min, add the tomato cans, chickpea can (throw the juice in there) and 1-2 cup of broth depending of how soupy you want it. Throw the chicken in the pot, Nest the chicken under some veggie, bring the thing to a boil. Taste to adjust seasoning if needed.

Once boiling, put the dutch oven in the oven (lid on!) for ~1h to 1h30.

Make semoule using broth.
