Chili Lime Chicken Sweet Potato Skillet

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 30 mins Servings: Serving: 1 Source: chiselandfork.com

Ingredients

2 tablespoon olive oil

2lb chicken thighs costco, diced into 1" cubes

Salt and pepper

~2 lb sweet potato, diced into ½" cubes

6-7 mini sweet pepper, seeds removed and sliced

2 small red onion, diced

8 garlic cloves, minced

2 tablespoon chili powder

2 tablespoon cumin

2 tablespoon paprika

½ teaspoon cayenne pepper

1 teaspoon kosher salt

1 cup low sodium chicken stock

3 limes zested

2 can black beans, rinsed and drained

½ cup cilantro leaves, chopped

avocado, sour cream, european yogurt, cheese, etc..

Description

Modified by tom

Directions

Toss chicken in some of the spices with some salt and pepper.

Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in large skillet over medium-high heat, cooking for 4 minutes per side or until golden brown. Remove the chicken and set aside.

Toss swzzt potatoes in avocado oil and salt and pepper and add to skillet (may need 2 skillets) and reduce heat to medium heat. cook for 10 minutes, tossing occasionally. Add bell peppers and red onion, garlic, lime zest, and cook for 5 minutes. At this point, the sweet potato should be browning.

Next, add in the spices, chicken stock and chicken. Stir so that everything is combined and then cover and cook for about 5 minutes or until sweet potatoes are cooked through.

Remove from heat and stir in the black beans. Adjust for seasoning. Garnish with cilantro, avocado and some lime juice. Serve immediately.

Notes

May need to do it in a large flat pot
