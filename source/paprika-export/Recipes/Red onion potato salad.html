Red onion potato salad

Prep Time: 10 Cook Time: 15 Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4

Ingredients

1/2 pounds potato

1 minced fennel bulb

1 chopped red onion

(decoration) celery leaves or aneth branch

salt and pepper


for the sauce:

5 floz mayonaise

2 cfsp mustard a l'ancienne

2 cfsp of chopped aneth

Directions

Cook potatoes in salted water for 10-15 min

Mix the fennel, celery, and onion in a large bowl. Separately, prepare the sauce by mixing mayonaise mustard and aneth together.

drain the potatoes, rince them under cold water, drain them again. Add the potatoes in the bowl and sprinkle with sauce, stir. Decorate with celery leave or aneth leaves. Serve
