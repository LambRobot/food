Tomates Farcies & Lentils

★★★★★

Ingredients

tomates farcies:

1 to 1.5lb ground beef (can be ground pork or veal)

6-8 medium size tomatoes (home grown!)

1 hand length of baguette, (size petit pain +-), cut in pieces

some milk (almond or other)

2 large garlic cloves

3/4 tbsp salt

1/2 tbsp ground pepper

1 to 1.5 tbsp herbes de provence


lentilles:

2cup lentils (1 pack smart and final)

1 medium onion

1 garlic clove

broth to cover

1/2 tbsp curry

1/2 salt

Directions

Preheat the oven at 400 F
Chop the bread and soak it in a deep dish of milk.
Cut open the tomatoes and empty the inside with a spoon and put them in a large metallic bowl. Crush it grossly with a puree masher. Keep track of the tomatoes “caps”.

Chop the onion. Heat some olive oil in the saute pan, add the onion + pressed garlic and cook on low.When translucent, add the crushed tomatoes innards and heat on high. Add salt pepper and curry.

Once the mixture is really hot and the solid bits start to break down, add the lentils and top up with broth. Get to a boil then reduce on low with lid slightly open. Check to make sure there is enough loquid without being too soupy. It will take 30-45 min to cook.

Meanwhile put the ground beef in a large mixing bowl and add the salt, pepper, herbes de provence, crushed garlic. Add the bread soaked in milk (should break down by hand), discard the extra milk. Mic everything until consistent.

Fill the tomatoes without overfilling. You can add some garlic powder or piment espelette on top of the meat, then Put their hat back. With the extra meat you can make a meat loaf. Put everything in a oven dish and bake for 30 min. Check with instant meat thermometer for 140F.

Done 🥳
