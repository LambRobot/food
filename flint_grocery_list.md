# 🛒 Grocery List — Fatty-Liver, Hotel Room (fridge + microwave)

*Grab-and-go, no real cooking. Shelf-stable stuff lives on the counter; only the cold items go in the tiny fridge.*
**One rule:** fish / nuts / yogurt / eggs / beans / veg / fruit / oats + water or black coffee = winning.

---

### 🥬 Produce
- [ ] **Apples / oranges / pears** — portable fruit; fiber blunts the sugar
- [ ] **Berries** (blueberries/strawberries) — low-sugar fruit for yogurt & oats
- [ ] **Bananas** — potassium, grab-and-go
- [ ] **Baby carrots, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, bell peppers** — no-prep veg to dip
- [ ] **Baby spinach / salad greens** — fiber; for wraps & bowls
- [ ] **Sweet potatoes** — *best* microwave carb here; high fiber, steadier blood sugar than bread/rice
- [ ] **Avocado** — healthy fat; mash onto a wrap
- [ ] **Lemon** — dress fish/veg with lemon + olive oil instead of sugary sauce

### 🥫 Canned / Pantry aisle
- [ ] **Canned/pouch sardines, salmon, or tuna** — ⭐ omega-3 fish is *protective* for the liver; zero prep
- [ ] **Ready-to-eat lentil / bean pouches** — plant protein + fiber; microwave & eat
- [ ] **Microwave quinoa / brown-rice pouches** — whole-grain base for a hot bowl
- [ ] **Rolled or quick oats** — soluble fiber; add hot water or microwave for breakfast
- [ ] **Olive oil** (small bottle) — the Mediterranean fat; dress everything
- [ ] **Natural nut butter** (single packets) — healthy fat + protein, no sugar added
- [ ] **Whole-grain crackers** — the swap for chips
- [ ] **Hummus** — chickpea = fiber + protein for dipping veg
- [ ] **Olives** (small tub) — healthy-fat snack

### 🧊 Dairy / Fridge (keep these cold)
- [ ] **Plain Greek yogurt** (0–2%, unsweetened) — high protein; breakfast or dessert with berries
- [ ] **Cottage cheese** — protein, low effort
- [ ] **Eggs** (or pre-boiled) — protein; "mug eggs" in the microwave
- [ ] **Feta or light string cheese** — moderate; a little adds flavor

### 🍗 Deli
- [ ] **Rotisserie chicken** — roasted hot protein; microwave a portion for 2–3 meals (easy on the skin)
- [ ] **Low-sodium sliced turkey** — quick protein for wraps

### 🌾 Bread aisle
- [ ] **Whole-grain wraps or bread** — fiber; far better than white bread/bagels

### 🥜 Snacks / Nuts
- [ ] **Walnuts** — ⭐ especially liver-friendly (omega-3)
- [ ] **Almonds / pistachios** (unsalted) — healthy fat + protein
- [ ] **Roasted chickpeas or dry-roasted edamame** — crunchy fiber + protein (chip replacement)
- [ ] **85% dark chocolate** — your "dessert," one square at a time
- [ ] **Plain / air-popped popcorn** — high-fiber, low-cal snack

### ❄️ Frozen (for the microwave)
- [ ] **Frozen edamame** or **steam-in-bag broccoli / green beans** — instant hot veg side

### 🥤 Drinks
- [ ] **Water + sparkling water** — your default
- [ ] **Unsweetened tea bags** — zero sugar
- [ ] *(Coffee — use the room maker; drink it **black**, it's liver-protective)*

---

### 🚫 Skip (the liver-fat traps)
Soda · juice · sweet tea · **sweetened yogurt & granola/protein bars** (check for <8g added sugar) ·
chips · cookies · pastries · white bread · sugary cereal · sweet beef jerky · **mini-bar alcohol**.

*⚕️ Educational, not medical advice.*
